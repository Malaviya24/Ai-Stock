## 1️⃣ Share on Net Sale (MOST IMPORTANT)

Formula:

```
Share on Net Sale = Annual Sales / Total Equity Shares
```

Interpretation:

Higher = more revenue backing per share.

Equivalent to:

```
Sales Per Share
```

---

## 2️⃣ 52 Week High / Low Ratio

```
Volatility Ratio = 52W High ÷ 52W Low
```

Interpretation:

Higher ratio = more volatile stock.
