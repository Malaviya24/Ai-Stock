# 📘 PART 1 — EXACT LOGIC EXTRACTION (NO STORY, ONLY SYSTEM)

## 🔹 Core Formula

[
SRTV = \frac{Current\ Price}{124\ DMA}
]

Where:

124 DMA = Average of last 124 trading day closes

---

## 🔹 Entry Rule (Start SIP)

If:

```
SRTV < 0.96
```

➡ ETF undervalued
➡ Start Monthly SIP (not lump sum)

---

## 🔹 Exit Rule (Book Profit)

If:

```
SRTV > 1.12
```

➡ ETF overvalued
➡ Exit position
➡ Rotate capital to lowest SRTV ETF

---

## 🔹 Strategy Nature

This is:

Mean Reversion Strategy
Using 124-day moving average as equilibrium
