# Supu Compounding Strategy

## 1. Objective

A rapid compounding strategy aimed at small capital growth by executing high-probability short-term trades.

## 2. Universe

- **NIFTY 50** or High Beta Stocks.

## 3. Logic Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Entry Condition

- **Timeframe:** Daily Candle.
- **Condition:** Buy when the stock closes above its **5-Day High**.
- **Momentum:** This signals a short-term breakout.

### Step 2: Exit Condition

- **Target:** Fixed small profit target (e.g., **3-5%**).
- **Stop Loss:** Below the low of the breakout candle.
- **Time Stop:** Exit if target not hit within 5 days.

### Step 3: Compounding

- **Rule:** Reinvest 100% of profits into the next trade.
- **Goal:** To double the capital in a specific number of trades (e.g., 20 successful trades).

## Summary

- **Focus:** Speed and Compounding.
- **Risk:** Higher risk due to full capital deployment.
- **Best For:** Aggressive traders with small accounts.
