# Weekly ETF Contrarian Strategy

## 1. Objective
A mean-reversion strategy that buys ETFs that have fallen the most over the last week, expecting them to bounce back.

## 2. Universe
*   **Liquid ETFs** (Nifty, Bank, IT, Gold, etc.).

## 3. Logic Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Calculate Weekly Performance
*   Measure the % Change of all ETFs over the **last 5 trading days**.

### Step 2: Rank by Weakness
*   Sort the list from **Worst Performing** to **Best Performing**.
*   **Top Picks:** The ETFs at the top of the list (e.g., down -4% or -5%).

### Step 3: Execution
*   **Buy:** The **Top 3** worst performers of the week.
*   **Logic:** "What goes down fast, often bounces fast." (Oversold condition).
*   **Hold Period:** Hold for **1 Week** (until next rebalancing) OR until a profit target (e.g., 3%) is hit.

## Summary
*   **Contrarian:** You are buying what everyone else is selling.
*   **Risk:** Sometimes a sector keeps falling. Diversification across 3 ETFs mitigates this.
*   **Best For:** Swing traders looking for short-term reversals.
