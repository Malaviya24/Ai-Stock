# Monthly Candle Strategy

## 1. Objective

A positional trading strategy that uses monthly price action to determine the long-term trend direction. It requires very little screen time (check once a month).

## 2. Universe

- **NIFTY 50** (Blue-chip stocks).

## 3. Logic Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Analyze Previous Month

- **High:** Previous Month's Highest Price.
- **Low:** Previous Month's Lowest Price.
- **Close:** Previous Month's Closing Price.

### Step 2: Determine Trend

- **Bullish:** If the current month's price crosses **ABOVE** the Previous Month's High.
- **Bearish:** If the current month's price crosses **BELOW** the Previous Month's Low.
- **Sideways:** If the price is stuck between the High and Low (Inside Bar).

### Step 3: Execution

- **Buy Signal:** Enter when the price breaks the previous month's high.
- **Stop Loss:** Below the previous month's low.
- **Sell Signal:** Exit when the price breaks below the previous month's low (reversal).

## Summary

- **Focus:** Monthly timeframe eliminates daily noise.
- **Action:** Check at the start of each month or set alerts for monthly highs/lows.
- **Best For:** Busy investors who want to ride long trends in quality stocks.
