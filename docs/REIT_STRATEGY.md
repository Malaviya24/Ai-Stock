# REIT & InvIT Strategy

## 1. Objective

A yield-focused strategy for investing in Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITs) and Infrastructure Investment Trusts (InvITs) to generate steady passive income.

## 2. Universe

- **All listed REITs & InvITs** in India (e.g., Embassy, Mindspace, PowerGrid InvIT).

## 3. Logic Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Yield Calculation

- **Dividend Yield:** Check the historical distribution per unit (DPU).
  - Target: **Yield > 6-7%** annually (tax-efficient).
- **Price Appreciation:** Check the NAV (Net Asset Value) trend.

### Step 2: Valuation Check

- **Price to NAV:** Compares Market Price to the Net Asset Value.
  - **Discount:** If Price < NAV, it's undervalued (Good Buy).
  - **Premium:** If Price > NAV, check if yield justifies the premium.

### Step 3: Stability Check

- **Occupancy Rate:** Must be > 85% (for REITs).
- **WALE (Weighted Average Lease Expiry):** Longer is better (> 4-5 years).
- **Debt:** Low Loan-to-Value (LTV) ratio (< 49%).

## Summary

- **Goal:** Capital preservation + Regular Income.
- **Action:** Buy on dips when yield crosses 7% or price falls below NAV.
- **Best For:** Retirement planning or low-risk allocation.
