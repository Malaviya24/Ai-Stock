# Marking Strategy

## 1. Objective
A visual strategy to identify critical support and resistance zones based on historical price action.

## 2. Universe
*   **Any Stock or Index.**

## 3. Logic Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Identify Key Levels
*   **Monthly Highs/Lows:** Strongest support/resistance.
*   **Weekly Highs/Lows:** Medium strength.
*   **Daily Highs/Lows:** Short-term strength.

### Step 2: Zone Creation
*   Create a "Zone" around these levels (e.g., ±1% range).
*   **Demand Zone:** Where price bounced up previously.
*   **Supply Zone:** Where price fell down previously.

### Step 3: Action
*   **Buy:** When price enters a Demand Zone and shows a reversal candle (Hammer/Green).
*   **Sell:** When price enters a Supply Zone and shows rejection (Shooting Star/Red).

## Summary
*   **Pure Price Action:** No indicators, just price levels.
*   **Context:** Helps you know *where* to trade, regardless of the strategy used.
