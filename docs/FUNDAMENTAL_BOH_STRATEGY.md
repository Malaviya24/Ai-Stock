# Fundamental + BOH Strategy

## 1. Objective
Combines **Fundamental Analysis** (Quality) with **Technical Analysis** (Momentum/BOH). It ensures you buy "Good Companies" only when they are in an "Uptrend".

## 2. Universe
*   **NIFTY 500**

## 3. Logic Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Fundamental Filter (The "Quality" Check)
*   **Profit Growth:** Must be > 10% YoY.
*   **ROE (Return on Equity):** Must be > 15%.
*   **Debt-to-Equity:** Must be < 1.0 (Low Debt).
*   **P/E Ratio:** Must be reasonable (< Industry Avg or historical median).

### Step 2: Technical Filter (The "Timing" Check)
*   **BOH Zone:** Apply the BOH (Breakout High) logic.
*   **Rule:** The stock must be in the **Green Zone** (Top 25% of 52-week range).

### Step 3: Execution
*   **Buy:** When a fundamentally strong stock enters the Green Zone.
*   **Sell:** When fundamentals deteriorate OR price drops into the Red Zone.

## Summary
*   **Double Confirmation:** Fundamentals ensure safety; Technicals ensure timing.
*   **Best For:** Medium to Long-term investors who want growth at a reasonable price (GARP).
